How The Two Modes Connect
Focused — Agent tabs in center pane. One agent at a time. Right pane = viewer.
Best for: debugging a specific agent, reviewing output, diving deep into one task

Overview — Orchestrator chat in center. Agent tiles in right pane (replaces viewer).
Best for: monitoring all agents at a glance, watching the orchestrator coordinate

Switching — prefix v toggles right pane mode. enter on a tile jumps to focused view of that agent.
Both modes share the same agent-tab-bar — always shows agent state LEDs for at-a-glance awareness
