Before · After

§01

The current frontmatter renderer is a straight port from the initial viewer spike — monospace key: value rows in a tinted box, no type awareness. The proposed replacement treats frontmatter as a typed inspector: enum values render as bracket chips with an accent rail, relations render as → Target pointers wired into the existing wiki-link click handler, and numeric / URL / date / boolean values each get their own vocabulary. Data is Aela, Goddess of Light from the Padarax vault.

🔆 Aela, Goddess of Light

Aela is revered as a figure of divine radiance and benevolence. Her existence and tales of her power have shaped the beliefs and traditions of many cultures across Padarax. She is depicted as a beacon of hope, wisdom, and protection, with the ability to banish darkness and guide lost souls.

Much of her origins were lost to time, but it is said she once walked the lands as a mortal. A great sorceress who banished Konosh, the Hunger from the mortal plane.

Physical Description

She is described as a figure of unparalleled beauty and radiance. Possessing an ethereal presence that captivates those who behold her. With golden hair that cascades down in soft waves, reminiscent of rays of sunlight.

The DM's Knowledge

When the Ancorani arrived on Padarax, their superior technology and mastery of arcane arts made them appear godlike. Her own physical form was damaged during the Shardfall, forcing her to upload her consciousness into the Aurospire.

Interaction notes

§04
[ READ MODE ] today — just renders
Click a relation — fires the existing link-click event, same as body wiki-links. No new plumbing; the inspector just emits a.wiki-link[data-path] anchors that attachWikiLinkHandlers picks up.

Click a chip — emits a filter-by event that the enclosing dashboard / search can consume. Optional; falls back to inert if no handler is attached.

Click a URL — opens externally, identical to the body handling.
[ EDIT MODE ] future — typed editing
Enter / i on a row — row becomes editable; cursor drops into the appropriate input widget (bracket field / dropdown / link autocomplete / date picker).

Typed field schemas — when dashboard.yml declares Pantheon → vault/Pantheons/Pantheons/, the relation picker autocompletes from just that folder. This is where the design pays off: the same visual shape in read mode and edit mode, with the type system constraining the input.

Esc / <CR> — commit the row back to the .md file, re-render through the same renderer. No mode flicker.
